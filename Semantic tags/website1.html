About Formula 1 2025 Regulations

The 2025 Formula 1 regulations aim to enhance the competitiveness and sustainability of the sport. Key changes include a shift towards more hybrid power units, increased aerodynamic efficiency, and a greater emphasis on cost control for teams.

Power Unit Changes

The new regulations will introduce a more powerful hybrid power unit, with a focus on improving energy recovery systems and reducing fuel consumption.
